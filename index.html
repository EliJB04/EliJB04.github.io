Problem statement: Campus Dining Times

Lines and wait times are often misrepresented, and students may miscalculate how much time they have to wait or have much spare time they have before their next class.

Affinity diagram: Ideas to Solve Slow Campus Dining Times

Our affinity diagram is broken down into sections based on important features we want to implicate into our app. We developed ideas that will help a user deide where they want to eat and what is more convienent to them.
